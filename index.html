Commonly asked questions.

Common questions about our treatments, from knee injections to health screening.

Do you offer knee pain treatment and knee injection therapy?

Yes. Our doctors provide intra-articular knee injections and trigger-point injections to relieve joint and muscle pain, alongside assessment and ongoing management of knee and joint conditions.

What is an intra-articular (knee) injection?

It is an injection given directly into the knee joint to reduce inflammation and ease pain. It is a quick, in-clinic procedure performed by our doctors, often used for arthritis and chronic knee pain.

Are you really open 24 hours?

Yes. Permai Polyclinics Fortuna is open 24 hours a day, every day of the year, for both routine and emergency care.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are welcome any time. You can also message us on WhatsApp at 012-939 3537 to arrange a convenient time.

Do you offer pre-employment health screening?

Yes. We provide pre-employment and general health screening packages, including blood pressure, blood glucose, urine tests and full blood tests.

Do you treat children and families?

Absolutely. We care for the whole family, from babies and children to adults and the elderly, including immunisations and growth check-ups.

Do you provide emergency treatment and IV drips?

Yes. We offer emergency treatment around the clock, along with injections and intravenous (IV) drips when needed.

Where is the clinic located?

We are at the Fortuna Commercial Centre, Mile 2.5, Jalan Penampang, Kota Kinabalu (Ground Floor, Lot 3, Block D2, Lorong Seroja 2).
